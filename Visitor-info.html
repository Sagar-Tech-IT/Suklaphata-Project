Welcome to Suklaphata!

Plan your own adventure! Choose your reason to visit, how long you’ll stay, and who you’re bringing along. The park will respond with a plan that fits you best no apps, no forms, just nature and creativity.

Wildlife Photography Adventure

Capture sunrise moments near the grasslands and wetlands. Perfect light, peaceful setting, and shy deer waiting for your lens.

Bird Watcher’s Paradise

Walk quietly along Suklaphata’s trails — watch Bengal Florican, owls, and kingfishers glide past under golden light.

Campfire Evenings

Set up near the Tharu community area. Watch fireflies after dusk, share stories, and let nature’s sounds take over.

The Explorer’s Trail

Hike through open fields and woodland edges. Every turn reveals something new — from peacocks to silent ponds.

Perfect for a day trip: Safari + Sunset viewpoint.

Before You Go

  • Carry reusable bottles keep Suklaphata clean.
  • Best time: October to April.
  • Hire a local guide for hidden trails and safe spotting.
  • Never miss sunrise safaris pure magic.
